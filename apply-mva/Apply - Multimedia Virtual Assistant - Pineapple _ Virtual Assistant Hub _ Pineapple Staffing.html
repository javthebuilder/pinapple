Qualifications

APPLICATION REQUIREMENTS Collapse

You will be successful in this role if you have the following qualifications:

• You are able to take constructive criticism positively and use that to improve your work and production techniques

• You have verified skills or experience in ANY of the following: video editing, motion graphics design, 3D animation, videography, graphic design, illustration, web design, audio design and production, original music production, and voice talent

• You have dedicated video editing / graphic design / motion graphics / 3D rendering / sound design software ie: Final Cut Pro, Adobe Premiere, Avid, Adobe Photoshop, Adobe Illustrator, Adobe Lightroom, Procreate, 3Ds Max, Maya, Z-Brush, Adobe Audition, Pro-Tools, Logic Pro, Ableton Live, Reason, FL Studio, etc

• You have a desktop or laptop computer that is powerful enough to run the above programs

• You are able to follow precise instructions and efficient with your time management

• You can speak and write well in English

• You can connect with a variety of people and build strong and friendly rapport

• You have good attention to detail especially in written reports, video conversations, text and e-mail

• You are comfortable with virtual technologies and can learn quickly

• Have reliable high-speed internet connection at least 10mbps

• Have a webcam and headset with microphone

• Able to work at night i.e. 11pm-8am (8am-5pm Arizona time)

HARDWARE AND SOFTWARE REQUIREMENTS Collapse

Applicants must meet the following requirements for hardware and software:

• Have a reliable high-speed internet connection (~10 mbps both download and upload)

• Must have wired connection - DSL or Fiber

• Have reliable computer hardware and software (will be verified by our tech team)
